How to Update Firmware Settings on Epson ET-2850?

Your complete guide to maintaining optimal performance with the latest firmware updates for your EcoTank printer.

Firmware is the permanent software programmed into your Epson ET-2850 EcoTank printer that controls how the device operates. It serves as the bridge between the printer's hardware and the software you interact with, managing everything from basic printing functions to advanced features like wireless connectivity and ink level monitoring.

Unlike regular software that runs on your computer, firmware is stored directly in the printer's memory. The Epson ET-2850, being part of the EcoTank series, receives periodic firmware updates that can significantly enhance your printing experience.

Why Firmware Updates Matter

Epson releases firmware updates for the ET-2850 to:

  • Fix known bugs and improve stability
  • Enhance existing features and add new functionality
  • Improve compatibility with new operating systems
  • Optimize printing quality and performance
  • Address security vulnerabilities
  • Extend the lifespan of your printer
Important: Never interrupt a firmware update once it has started. Power failures or disconnections during update can render your printer inoperable. Ensure stable power and network connections before beginning.

Benefits of Updating Your ET-2850 Firmware

Keeping your Epson ET-2850 firmware up-to-date provides numerous advantages that enhance your printing experience and protect your investment:

Improved Print Quality

Updates often include refinements to the printing algorithms, resulting in sharper text, more accurate colors, and better photo reproduction.

Enhanced Connectivity

Wireless performance improvements and new connection options are frequently added through firmware updates.

New Features

Epson sometimes introduces completely new functionality through firmware updates, expanding what your printer can do.

Bug Fixes

Issues like paper jams, connectivity drops, or incorrect ink level readings are often resolved through firmware patches.

Security Patches

Critical security updates protect your printer from potential vulnerabilities in networked environments.

Extended Compatibility

Stay compatible with new operating system versions and mobile devices as they're released.

How Often Should You Update?

Epson typically releases firmware updates for the ET-2850 every 3-6 months, or as needed to address specific issues. It's recommended to:

  • Check for updates when you first set up your printer
  • Check quarterly for maintenance updates
  • Update immediately if you're experiencing issues that a firmware update might resolve
  • Update before installing a new operating system on your computer

How to Update Firmware on Epson ET-2850

Epson provides multiple methods to update your ET-2850's firmware. Choose the one that best suits your setup:

Method 1: Automatic Update via Epson Software

1
Prepare Your Printer

Ensure your ET-2850 is turned on and connected to your computer via USB or the same network. Have at least 50% ink in all tanks.

2
Open Epson Software

Launch Epson Printer Utility (Mac) or Epson Event Manager (Windows) that came with your printer.

3
Check for Updates

Look for a "Firmware Update" or "Product Updates" option in the software. The exact location varies by operating system.

4
Download and Install

If an update is available, follow the prompts to download and install it. The process may take 5-15 minutes.

5
Complete the Update

Your printer will restart automatically. Don't turn it off or disconnect during this process.

Method 2: Manual Update via Epson Website

1
Visit Epson Support

Go to Epson's official support website and navigate to the ET-2850 product page.

2
Download Firmware

Locate the firmware section and download the latest version for your operating system.

3
Run the Installer

Double-click the downloaded file and follow the on-screen instructions to update your printer.

Method 3: Update via Printer Panel

1
Access Settings

On your ET-2850's control panel, navigate to Settings > General Settings > Update.

2
Check for Updates

Select "Check Now" to search for available firmware updates (requires active internet connection).

3
Install Update

If an update is found, confirm installation and wait for the process to complete.

Note: The printer panel method requires your ET-2850 to be connected to WiFi or Ethernet. For USB-only connections, use Methods 1 or 2.

Troubleshooting Firmware Update Issues

Update Fails to Start

  • Ensure your printer is connected properly (USB, WiFi, or Ethernet)
  • Check that you have sufficient ink levels (all tanks above 50%)
  • Restart both your computer and printer before trying again
  • Disable any firewall or antivirus software temporarily
  • Try a different USB cable if using wired connection

Update Stalls or Freezes

  • Don't turn off the printer - some updates take longer than expected
  • Ensure stable power supply (consider UPS for power fluctuations)
  • For wireless updates, move printer closer to the router
  • Wait at least 30 minutes before assuming it's stuck
  • If truly frozen, you may need service center assistance

Printer Not Working After Update

  • Restart the printer and your computer
  • Reinstall printer drivers from Epson's website
  • Check for a newer firmware update that might fix issues
  • Reset network settings if experiencing connectivity problems
  • As last resort, perform a factory reset (Settings > Reset)

Cannot Find Update Option

  • Ensure you're using the latest version of Epson software
  • Some regions distribute updates differently - check local Epson site
  • Your current firmware might already be up-to-date
  • Older operating systems might not support newest firmware

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my current firmware version on ET-2850?

On the printer's control panel, go to Settings > General Settings > Device Information. Alternatively, in Epson software on your computer, look under Printer Information or similar section.

Can I downgrade firmware if the new version causes problems?

Epson typically doesn't support downgrading firmware. New updates often include security patches and improvements that can't be reversed. If you're having issues, check for a newer update that might fix them.

Does firmware update affect my ink cartridges or warranty?

No, firmware updates won't void your warranty or affect genuine Epson ink. However, some updates may improve ink level detection which could affect third-party cartridges.

Why does my ET-2850 need internet for firmware updates?

While internet isn't strictly required (you can download updates manually), automatic updates need internet to check Epson's servers for the latest firmware version and security patches.

How long does a firmware update typically take?

Most ET-2850 firmware updates complete in 5-15 minutes, depending on the update size and your connection speed. The printer will restart automatically when done.

  • Maintain adequate ink levels before updating
